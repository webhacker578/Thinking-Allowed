Thinking allowed/aloud is a platform for Indian students of all ages to express their views to a global audience. Our aim is to encourage all students to see themselves as active participants in their country, to believe that their opinions are valid and should be heard. We want to encourage students to be able to present well thought out arguments on the myriad issues in their lives. Through informed debate we hope to encourage students to recognise that others can have opinions different to their own which are equally valid. Thinking allowed/aloud hopes to engender in students, a realisation of the power of language and communication. A platform where all voices can be heard.

Please join us.
